How much does a ticket to ride the Metropolitano de Lisboa cost?

Carris/Metro single ticket: 1,45€ (valid for only one trip in all the Metropolitano and Carris networks, during 1 hours after the initial validation)

Carris/Metro (24h): 6,15€

Carris/Metro/Transtejo (Cacilhas) (24h): 9,15€

Carris/Metro/CP (24h): 10,15€

7 Colinas/Viva Viagem card: 0,50€
